Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Neha Sharma’s film Jogira Sara Ra Ra is not doing well at the box office. It is struggling to mint a mere total of Rs 2 crore.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

By Grace Cyril: Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Neha Sharma’s latest release Jogira Sara Ra Ra is a huge disaster. The film has proved to be a box office dud. In four days, Jogira Sara Ra Ra failed to even cross Rs 2 crore.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

JOGIRA SARA RA RA BOX OFFICE COLLECTION
Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Neha Sharma’s Jogira Sara Ra Ra released on May 26 in theatres. The film received mixed reviews from critics. On Day 1, the film minted just Rs 50 lakh, which is very less. On Day 4, May 29, the film reportedly minted just Rs 32 lakh. The total collection now stands at Rs 1.83 crore.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

ABOUT JOGIRA SARA RA RA
Jogira Sara Ra Ra stars Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Neha Sharma in lead roles. It is produced by Naeem A Siddiqui of Touchwood Multimedia Creations. It has Kiran Shyam Shroff as its creative producer. The film released in theatres on May 26. Directed by Kushan Nandy, the family comedy also stars Sanjay Mishra and Mahaakshay Chakraborty.
